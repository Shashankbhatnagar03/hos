About Sukriti Hospital & Research Center

Sukriti Hospital & Research Center is a premier multi-specialty healthcare facility located in the heart of Gomti Nagar, Lucknow. As a unit of Sudharas Healthcare Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to delivering world-class medical services with a focus on patient-centric care, advanced technology, and compassionate treatment.

Established with the vision to provide accessible and affordable healthcare, our hospital features state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modern OPDs, ICUs, diagnostic labs, and specialized research centers. Our team of highly qualified doctors and support staff ensures holistic care for patients of all ages.
